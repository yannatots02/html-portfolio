Adobong Manok Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 1 head garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce, garlic, and peppercorns for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Saut√© sliced onions in cooking oil until soft.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Pour in vinegar and let it boil for a few minutes.
  5. Stir in soy sauce, bay leaves, and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Pour in water and simmer until chicken is tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Simmer until the sauce thickens and chicken is fully cooked.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice.
