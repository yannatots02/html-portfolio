The Best Movies according to me!

My top 3 movies of the year 2023-2024.

The Pope Exorcist

This movie is all about exorcism, it's so much scary. I love the role of the pope. A detailed portrait of a priest who performed more than 100,000 exorcisms in his lifetime.

Wonka

This movie is all about making chocolates.It is the story of Willy Wonka, a character in the 1964 novel Charlie and the Chocolate Factory by Roald Dahl.

The Rewind

This movie is all about Mary loves John her entire life. After years of marriage, John's priorities change, straining their relationship and leading to a tragic accident that takes Mary's life. An extraordinary proposition allows John to rewind time.
